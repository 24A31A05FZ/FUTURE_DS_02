## BUSINESS CONTEXT

Customer churn is one of the most critical challenges facing subscription-based businesses today. In the SaaS and telecom sector, acquiring a new customer costs **5–7× more** than retaining an existing one. This analysis examines behavioral and transactional data from **7,043 customers** to identify churn drivers and prescribe evidence-based retention strategies.

## TOP FINDINGS

### 1. Contract Type Is the #1 Churn Predictor
- Month-to-month contracts have a **42.7% churn rate**
- One-year contracts: **11.3% churn rate**
- Two-year contracts: only **2.8% churn rate**
- **Recommendation:** Aggressively incentivize annual/biennial upgrades

### 2. Early Tenure = Highest Risk
- 50%+ of churned customers leave within their **first 12 months**
- The critical intervention window is **Month 1–3**
- **Recommendation:** Implement an onboarding success program

### 3. Payment Method Signals Intent
- Electronic check users churn at **45.3%** — nearly double the average
- Credit card/bank transfer users show the lowest churn
- **Recommendation:** Nudge customers toward auto-pay methods

### 4. Fiber Optic Internet Has Paradoxically High Churn
- Despite being the premium tier, Fiber Optic has **41.9% churn**
- Suggests unmet expectations or poor value perception
- **Recommendation:** Improve Fiber support quality and set clear expectations

### 5. Missing Add-on Services = Churn Risk
- Customers without Online Security churn at **41.8%** vs. **14.6%** with it
- TechSupport absence leads to **41.6%** churn
- **Recommendation:** Bundle security & support into base plans

## STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS (Top 5)

1. **Launch Contract Conversion Campaign** — Offer 15–20% discount for month-to-month → annual upgrades
2. **Deploy Onboarding Success Program** — Assign Customer Success Managers to all new accounts (first 90 days)
3. **Implement Predictive Churn Alerts** — Use risk scores to trigger proactive outreach before cancellation
4. **Bundle Security & Support** — Include OnlineSecurity + TechSupport in all base plans to reduce churn by ~12%
5. **Auto-Pay Incentive Program** — Provide $5/month discount for customers on bank transfer or credit card

## CONCLUSION

This analysis reveals a clear, actionable churn reduction roadmap. By targeting the **high-risk segment** with personalized interventions and incentivizing **long-term contracts**, the business can realistically reduce churn from 26.5% to under 18% within two quarters — representing **$500,000+ in annual revenue protection**.
